Care Instructions

Washing

Machine wash cold with like colors. Use mild detergent and avoid bleach.

Drying

Tumble dry low or line dry in shade. Avoid direct sunlight to preserve colors.

Ironing

Iron on medium heat while slightly damp. Use pressing cloth for delicate fabrics.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place. Fold carefully or hang to prevent wrinkles.
